About Me

I am an Epidemiologist and Emergency Operations Leader with over 15 years of progressive experience managing state and national-scale public health operations. My work spans Advocacy, Communication, and Social Mobilization (ACSM), immunization campaign delivery, and outbreak response within complex emergency settings.

Currently serving as a Field Coordinator & NEOC Focal Person for AFENET-NSTOP in Nigeria, I lead operations planning and analyze data to improve program quality and equity. I am passionate about leveraging technology to reach underserved and high-risk populations.
